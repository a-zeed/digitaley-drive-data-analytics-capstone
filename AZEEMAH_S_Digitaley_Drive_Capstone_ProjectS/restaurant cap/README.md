# 🍽️ Restaurant Ratings & Consumer Preference Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyzes restaurant ratings, customer demographics, cuisine preferences, and consumer behavior to uncover factors that influence restaurant performance and customer satisfaction.

The analysis helps restaurant owners and investors understand customer needs and identify business growth opportunities.

---

## Business Problem

Restaurants operate in highly competitive environments where customer satisfaction directly affects profitability.

This project seeks to answer:

- Which restaurants receive the highest ratings?
- Which cuisines perform best?
- How do demographics influence ratings?
- What opportunities exist for restaurant investment?

## Key Insights

### Restaurant Performance

- Several restaurants consistently outperformed competitors.
- Top-rated restaurants demonstrated stronger customer alignment.

### Consumer Preferences

- Customer preferences significantly influenced restaurant ratings.

### Occupation Analysis

- Employed consumers generally provided higher ratings than students and unemployed consumers.

### Satisfaction Distribution

- High satisfaction ratings represented the largest segment of customer feedback.

### Cuisine Analysis

- Certain cuisines consistently attracted stronger ratings and customer demand.

---

## Recommendations

1. Invest in high-performing cuisine categories.
2. Target working professionals through marketing campaigns.
3. Improve service quality in underperforming restaurants.
4. Align menus with customer preferences.
5. Benchmark top-performing restaurants.
