# ✈️ Dano Airlines Passenger Satisfaction & Service Quality Analysis

## Project Overview

Customer satisfaction is a key performance indicator in the airline industry. This project analyzes passenger satisfaction data to identify the factors that influence customer experience and provide actionable recommendations for service improvement.

The analysis explores passenger demographics, travel characteristics, service quality ratings, and flight delays to understand their impact on overall satisfaction.

---

## Business Problem

Dano Airlines wants to understand:

- What drives passenger satisfaction?
- How do delays affect customer experience?
- Which customer segments are most satisfied?
- Which services have the greatest influence on satisfaction?

The goal is to improve customer retention and enhance overall service quality.

## Key Insights

### Customer Satisfaction

- Overall Satisfaction Rate: 43.45%
- More than half of passengers reported dissatisfaction.

### Travel Class Analysis

- Business Class passengers reported significantly higher satisfaction than Economy passengers.

### Travel Purpose

- Business travelers showed higher satisfaction levels than personal travelers.

### Delay Impact

- Satisfaction decreased as delays increased.
- On-time flights recorded the highest satisfaction rates.

### Service Quality Drivers

Top service drivers included:

- Seat Comfort
- Online Boarding
- Cleanliness
- In-flight Entertainment

---

## Recommendations

1. Reduce operational delays.
2. Improve Economy Class experience.
3. Invest in seat comfort and cleanliness.
4. Enhance digital boarding processes.
5. Improve communication during delays.
