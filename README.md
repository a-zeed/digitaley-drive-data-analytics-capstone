## Capstone Projects

### Project 1: Airline Passenger Satisfaction Analysis

Objective:
Analyze passenger satisfaction and identify key drivers of customer experience within an airline environment.

Key Deliverables:
- Interactive Power BI Dashboard
- KPI Development
- Customer Segmentation
- Delay Impact Analysis
- Service Quality Analysis

Key Insights:
- Satisfaction Rate: 43.45%
- Business Class passengers showed the highest satisfaction levels.
- Delays significantly reduced customer satisfaction.
- Seat Comfort, Online Boarding, and Cleanliness were major service drivers.

---

### Project 2: Restaurant Ratings & Consumer Preference Analysis

Objective:
Analyze restaurant performance, customer demographics, and consumer preferences to uncover business growth opportunities.

Key Deliverables:
- Interactive Power BI Dashboard
- Customer Behavior Analysis
- Restaurant Performance Evaluation
- Consumer Preference Insights

Key Insights:
- High-rated restaurants consistently outperformed competitors.
- Consumer preferences strongly influenced ratings.
- Working professionals provided higher average ratings.
- Certain cuisine categories demonstrated stronger market demand.

---

### Project 3: NYC Road Safety & Collision Intelligence Dashboard

Objective:
Analyze traffic collision records to identify accident hotspots, injury patterns, and contributing factors.

Key Deliverables:
- Interactive Power BI Dashboard
- Road Safety Analysis
- Accident Trend Analysis
- Geographic Risk Assessment

Key Insights:
- 238K collisions analyzed.
- Brooklyn and Queens recorded the highest accident volumes.
- Driver distraction emerged as a leading contributing factor.
- Passenger vehicles dominated collision involvement.
